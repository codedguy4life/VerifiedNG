Building Trust Across Nigeria

VerifiedNG was born from a simple problem — finding reliable, honest service providers in Nigeria is hard. We are changing that.

Our Story

VerifiedNG started with a frustration familiar to every Nigerian — asking five people for recommendations and still ending up with a plumber who doesn't show up, a tailor who misses your deadline, or an electrician whose work causes more damage than it fixes.

We built VerifiedNG to solve this. Not by creating another directory — but by creating a system of genuine community verification. Every provider on VerifiedNG is vouched for by someone in their community — a former employer, pastor, community leader or neighbour. Real accountability. Real trust.

We launched in Lagos and are rapidly expanding to cover every corner of Nigeria.

Our Mission

To connect every Nigerian with trusted, community-verified local service providers — and to help skilled Nigerians build sustainable businesses through honest work and real reputation.

Our Values

Trust First

Every provider is verified before going live. No exceptions.

Community

Verification comes from real people who know the provider personally.

Transparency

Real reviews. Real ratings. No fake stars or paid placements.

Nigerian Pride

Built in Nigeria, for Nigerians. Celebrating skilled people everywhere.

Why VerifiedNG?

For customers: Stop relying on guesswork. Every provider has been vetted by their community and rated by real customers. You see honest profiles, honest reviews, and honest prices.

For providers: Your skills deserve to be found. VerifiedNG gives honest, hardworking Nigerians a professional platform to showcase their abilities and grow their clientele beyond their street or neighbourhood.
